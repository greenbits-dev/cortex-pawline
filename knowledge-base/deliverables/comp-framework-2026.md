# Compensation Framework 2026

Pawline's compensation framework for 2026. Owner: [[lisa-rodriguez]] (CPO). Audience: regional leadership + practice managers + corporate roles. (Specific clinic-level comp ranges are in `internal/compensation/` — exec/regional-leader-only.)

## Framework structure

- **DVMs**: ProSal model with regional adjustments (changing in 2027 per [[2026-04-25-comp-restructure-2027]] proposal)
- **Practice Managers**: base + clinic-performance bonus + equity (Senior Associate equivalent)
- **Technicians**: hourly + skills-based level progression + retention bonuses
- **Client-services staff**: hourly + tenure-based level progression
- **RMD / ROD**: salary + equity-heavy
- **Director-level corporate roles**: salary + bonus + equity

## DVM ProSal mechanics

- **Base draw**: weekly draw, sized at ~75th-percentile-of-market for region + experience level
- **Production share**: 22-25% of personal production
- **Switch logic**: weekly, whichever is higher (base or production share)
- **Equity**: separate from comp framework, see [[equity-participation-program]]

## Comp adjustments

- **Annual cycle**: Q1 review with Q2 implementation
- **Off-cycle**: promotions, market correction, retention adjustments
- **Acquisition transitions**: acquired-clinic DVMs can grandfather their previous comp for up to 24 months, then convert to Pawline standard

## Regional cost-of-living adjustments

- **CA premium**: +12-18% over baseline
- **AZ / NV / TX**: baseline
- **CO / UT**: -3% to -6% (below baseline)
- **Reviewed annually** against external comp benchmarks (AVMA + practice-manager salary surveys)
