## What we're saying yes to (in 2026)

Targets meeting all four:
- Cluster-fit (per [[regional-cluster-economics]])
- Defensible EBITDA per [[ebitda-add-backs-policy]]
- Cultural fit (chart-audit pass + owner-DVM willing to stay 24+ months)
- Multiple in 5.5-9x defended-EBITDA band

## What we're saying no to (in 2026)

- Geographically isolated targets that don't enable a cluster
- Aggressive add-back posture from sellers
- Owner-DVM unwilling to stay through integration
- Pricing competition above 9x adjusted EBITDA
