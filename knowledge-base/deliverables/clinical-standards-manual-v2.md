# Clinical Standards Manual v2

Pawline's bound version of the Standard of Care protocol library. The canonical clinical reference. Owner: [[dr-elena-vasquez]] (CMO). Distributed to every Pawline DVM and bound copies in every clinic.

## Contents (table of contents)

1. **Foreword** — Sarah Okafor + Elena Vasquez
2. **Vault structure** — how to read this manual, when SOC applies vs DVM discretion
3. **Triage acuity** — [[triage-acuity-levels]]
4. **Preventive care protocols** (~30 protocols)
   - Vaccination schedules (canine, feline, exotics)
   - Parasite prevention
   - Wellness exam structure
5. **Sick-visit protocols** (~50 protocols)
   - Common GI presentations
   - Otitis (external, media)
   - Lameness workup
   - Dermatology common presentations
6. **Dental** — [[dental-grading-system]] + treatment branching by grade (~10 protocols)
7. **Surgical pathways** (~20 protocols)
   - Routine: spay, neuter, dental extractions, mass removals
   - Anesthesia tier mapping per procedure
8. **Anesthesia** — [[anesthesia-tier-policy]] + monitoring requirements (~15 protocols)
9. **Pain management** — multimodal analgesia by case type (~8 protocols)
10. **Critical-case triage and stabilization** (~10 protocols)
11. **Documentation standards** — what every chart must contain
12. **Coding alignment** — protocol-step-to-Cornerstone-code mapping (appendix)

## What's not in this manual

- Specialty cases (cardiology, internal medicine, neurology, etc.) — those follow specialty-society guidelines
- Atypical presentations — DVM judgment
- Anything explicitly outside the SOC envelope per [[clinical-protocols-policy]]

## Versioning

- v1 published 2024-Q3 (~80 protocols)
- v2 published 2026-02-18 (~140 protocols, anesthesia tightened, dental coded, charge capture aligned)
- v3 in scoping for late 2027 — likely additions: AI-scribe-augmented protocols, expanded specialty referral pathways

## How updates happen

- Quarterly proposed-revisions cycle, RMDs and Director of Clinical Quality propose, CMO signs
- Mid-cycle hot-fixes for safety issues
- Rollout to clinics: digital + bound update inserts; technicians + DVMs trained on changes within 30 days

## Distribution

- Every Pawline DVM (digital + printed copy at hire)
- Every clinic (bound copy in the prep area)
- Searchable digital version in Cornerstone help system + Pawline intranet
