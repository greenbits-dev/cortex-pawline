# 2026 Annual Operating Plan

The current-year plan: top-line revenue + EBITDA targets, regional breakdown, key initiatives. Approved at the Q4 2025 board, refreshed at the Q1 2026 board. Owner: [[tom-brennan]] (financial), [[marcus-kim]] (operational).

## Top-line targets

| Metric | 2025 actual | 2026 plan | Delta |
|--------|-------------|-----------|-------|
| Revenue | $241M | $268M | +11.2% |
| EBITDA margin | 17.0% | 17.5% | +50 bps |
| New clinics added | 14 | 9-11 | -5 to -3 (reflects [[2026-01-22-q3-ma-pause]]) |
| Total clinic count (year-end) | 80 | 89-91 | +9 to +11 |

## Regional breakdown

| Region | 2025 revenue | 2026 plan | Drivers |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| West (CA + NV) | $97M | $107M | Organic growth + 3 acquisitions H1 |
| Mountain (CO + UT) | $48M | $56M | Organic growth + 2-3 acquisitions H1 |
| Southwest (AZ + TX) | $96M | $105M | Organic growth + 4-5 acquisitions H1; Q3 pause limits H2 |

## Key initiatives

1. **PIMS migration program** — 12 migrations on schedule ([[pims-migration-pathway]])
2. **Clinical Protocol v2 adoption** — full network compliance by end of Q2 ([[2026-02-18-clinical-protocol-v2-publish]])
3. **West Region ROD seat fill** — close the dyad gap ([[2025-11-10-medical-director-dyad-rollout]])
4. **Telehealth pilot decision** — extend or hold by end of Q3 ([[2025-08-04-launch-telehealth-pilot]])
5. **AI clinical scribe pilot** — vendor selection Q2, pilot Q3 ([[2026-04-30-ai-clinical-scribe-vendor]])
6. **Q3 M&A pause** — integration debt resolution ([[2026-01-22-q3-ma-pause]])
7. **Comp restructure pilot** — 2 clinic pilot in Q4 2026 ([[2026-04-25-comp-restructure-2027]])

## Strategic context

This plan is the second year of the 5-year horizon set in [[2026-2030-strategic-plan]]. The [[2026-01-22-q3-ma-pause]] is a deliberate slowing of the deployment pace to protect quality through 2026, with the expectation of resumed pace in 2027.

## Risks

- [[texas-acquisition-pipeline-thinning]] — TX deployment may underdeliver
- West ROD vacancy — operational risk if not filled by mid-year
- Macroeconomic — vet services have been resilient; we don't model recession explicitly but watch consumer-spending indicators
