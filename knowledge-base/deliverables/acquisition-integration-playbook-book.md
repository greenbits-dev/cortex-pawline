# Acquisition Integration Playbook (bound version)

The bound, distributable version of the [[acquisition-integration-playbook]] given to regional leadership and the integration team. The playbook itself lives in `playbooks/`; this deliverable is the printed, role-card-formatted version.

## What's in the bound version

- **Foreword** — Marcus Kim + James Okwu
- **The 5 stages** — T-30 / T-day / T+30 / T+90 / T+180 ([[integration-stages]])
- **Role cards** — for the integration lead, RMD, ROD, practice manager, clinical lead
- **Function-by-function checklists** — clinical, ops, finance, IT, people
- **Exception protocols** — when a milestone slips, who escalates to whom and on what timeline
- **Communication templates** — staff letters, client letters, vendor notification scripts
- **Operational integration runbook** — payroll cutover, banking cutover, DEA registration handoff, vendor account transitions

## Distribution

- Every regional dyad (RMD + ROD)
- Every Area Operations Manager
- The corp dev / integration team
- The CMO and COO offices
- One copy per acquired clinic (during the integration period; reclaimed at T+180)

## Update cadence

- Annual revision led by the Director of Integration
- Mid-cycle hot-fixes when a recurring failure mode is detected (e.g., the 2024 mid-cycle update added the [[pims-migration-temp-staffing-required]] line item)

## Why this is bound and printed

The integration period is high-pressure and the digital-only version gets ignored under stress. The physical role-card format means a practice manager in their second week as a Pawline clinic has something on their desk to refer to. We've found this matters.
