# Integration stages (T-30 / T-day / T+30 / T+90 / T+180)

Pawline's integration framework, applied to every acquisition. The stages are owned cross-functionally — clinical, ops, finance, IT, people each have stage-specific milestones in [[acquisition-integration-playbook]].

## The stages

### T-30 (30 days pre-close)

- **Communication plan**: seller, staff, clients (post-close announcement only)
- **Day-1 readiness**: payroll cutover (ADP migration prep), banking setup, lease assignment
- **Clinical**: chart audit baseline; controlled-substance log handover plan
- **IT**: Cornerstone tenant provisioned (or PIMS migration timeline locked if migrating later)
- **People**: comp matching analysis; equity grants prepared for retained DVMs

### T-day (close)

- Wire transfer
- Banking cutover
- Payroll system cutover (ADP live)
- DEA registration handoff (per-clinic)
- Internal staff announcement; client-facing announcement timed per the comms plan

### T+30 (30 days post-close)

- **Operational**: practice manager + ROD walk-through; identify quick-win operational fixes
- **Clinical**: first chart audit; SOC protocol orientation for DVMs
- **Finance**: first NetSuite period close; verify P&L rollup is correct
- **IT**: data warehouse ingestion verified; legacy PIMS clinics on the migration schedule
- **People**: first all-hands in-person from a Pawline exec (often [[marcus-kim]] or [[dr-elena-vasquez]])

### T+90 (90 days post-close)

- **Operational**: clinic in standard ops cadence ([[2026-04-22-weekly-ops-review]] participation)
- **Clinical**: SOC protocol compliance check; anesthesia tier compliance check
- **Finance**: legacy AR cleanup substantially complete; collection ratio normalizing
- **People**: 90-day check-in with retained DVMs; equity grants land
- **Watch**: month-3 revenue dip (per [[acquired-clinic-revenue-dip-month-3]]) is at this stage — RMD/ROD intervention often needed

### T+180 (180 days post-close)

- **Clinical**: full participation in [[clinical-quality-review]]
- **Brand**: Pawline brand integration substantially complete (signage, digital, materials) — [[Pawline Endorsement]] decision finalized
- **Operational**: clinic indistinguishable from same-tier organic clinics in operating cadence
- **Finance**: clinic in steady-state P&L

## Why the stages matter

A pattern of slipping at any single stage propagates. The [[acquisition-integration-playbook]] is structured around this framework, with explicit accountability per stage by function.
