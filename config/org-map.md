## Reporting cadence

- **Daily**: Regional ops managers ping COO if any clinic posts <85% of forecast
- **Weekly (Tuesday)**: Ops review with COO + Regional VPs ([[2026-04-22-weekly-ops-review]])
- **Monthly (3rd Wed)**: Clinical Council with CMO + RMDs + Director of Clinical Quality
- **Monthly (2nd Wed)**: M&A Pipeline Review with Head of M&A + CFO + CEO
- **Quarterly**: Quarterly Business Review (regional VPs to exec team)
- **Quarterly**: Larkspur board meeting
- **Annual**: Leadership offsite (Q2), Finance Strategy Day (Q1)
