# Strategic Pricing 2026

Pricing analysis: Pawline's price positions vs competitors, planned changes, segment-level sensitivity. Audience: exec team only. Specific competitor-by-competitor benchmarking is the most sensitive content — broad distribution creates legal antitrust exposure.

## Current price posture

- Pawline GP visit pricing: ~mid-market for region (10-15% premium to local independents on average; ~5-10% discount to Banfield in shared markets)
- Pawline dental pricing: at-or-slightly-above local independent average; at parity with Banfield
- Pawline surgical pricing: aligned with Tier A flagship competitive positioning; specialty pricing per market

## 2026 planned price actions

- Wellness exam: 4% increase, effective 2026-04 (rolled out)
- Surgery: no general increase; selective procedure-level reviews
- Dental: structural change tied to [[2026-02-18-clinical-protocol-v2-publish]] coding alignment, not a price-list change
- Urgent care: no general change; the [[2026-04-12-fixed-fee-pricing-pilot]] would change everything in 6 clinics
